Word Split

Each 8-letter word is split into two 4-letter halves. Select two halves to reassemble a valid word.

Single Player
  • Each level, match 6 words in 30 seconds.
  • Clear as many levels as you can.
  • Words getter harder after each successful level.
2-Player
  • Study the grid.
  • First player to accept it has 15s to clear it.
  • Succeed to score a point; fail and your opponent scores.
  • First to 3 points wins the match.
